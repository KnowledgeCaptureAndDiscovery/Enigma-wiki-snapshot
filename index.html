Welcome to the ENIGMA Organic Data Science wiki!

This is a demonstration prototype for the Organic Data Science wiki for the ENIGMA collaboration.

The site was set up on December 10, 2014.

For any questions or to get an account on this wiki, please contact us at organic.data.science@gmail.com.

Before you start: About ODS and the Semantic Media Wiki

Anyone can edit this wiki to add information about their own site, their participation in ENIGMA Working Groups, and the status of their work. Before you can make edits you will be asked to go through a training session for semantic wikis, since this is a special wiki that has semantic annotations and reasoning. You can also go through the documentation here

To learn the basics of how wikis work, consult our Quick Guide to Using a Wiki, or the official User's Guide.

Consult the User's Guide for information on using the wiki software.
